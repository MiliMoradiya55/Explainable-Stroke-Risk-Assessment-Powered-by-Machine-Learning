Understanding Stroke Risk

Key Risk Factors
  • Age: Risk increases significantly after 55.
  • Hypertension: High blood pressure is the #1 contributor.
  • Heart Disease: Existing conditions like atrial fibrillation elevate risk.
  • Diabetes: High glucose levels damage blood vessels.
  • Obesity: BMI over 30 increases risk.
  • Smoking: Damages vessel walls and promotes clots.
How Our AI Works

We use a Random Forest classifier trained on a dataset of over 5,000 patients. The model analyzes your inputs and compares them with patterns learned from historical data to predict stroke probability.

Accuracy: Our model achieves ~85% accuracy on test data. However, this is for educational purposes only. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
